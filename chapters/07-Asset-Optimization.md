# Chapter 7: Asset Optimization

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Optimizing assets is fundamental to achieving target performance. This chapter covers best practices for all asset types.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [7.1 Texture Optimization](../subjects/07-01-Texture-Optimization.md)
Optimizing textures including resolution guidelines by platform, mipmap generation, texture atlasing, virtual texturing, texture streaming, sparse textures, filtering modes, wrapping modes, and RenderTexture management.

### [7.2 Mesh Optimization](../subjects/07-02-Mesh-Optimization.md)
Mesh optimization techniques including polygon count reduction, vertex welding, mesh LOD generation, mesh compression, index buffer optimization, vertex cache optimization, and triangle strip conversion.

### [7.3 Shader Optimization](../subjects/07-03-Shader-Optimization.md)
Shader performance optimization including variant stripping, shader compilation, shader warmup, uber shader vs multiple shaders, and shader complexity analysis.

### [7.4 Animation Optimization](../subjects/07-04-Animation-Optimization.md)
Animation data optimization including animation compression, animation culling, skeleton LOD, animation streaming, and animation instancing.

### [7.5 Audio Asset Optimization](../subjects/07-05-Audio-Asset-Optimization.md)
Audio optimization strategies including sample rate reduction, audio clip loading, streaming vs compressed in memory, and force to mono.
