# Chapter 9: Asset Format Guidelines

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Choosing the right asset format for each platform is critical for performance and quality. This chapter provides platform-specific recommendations.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [9.1 PC Platform](../subjects/09-01-PC-Platform.md)
Asset format recommendations for PC including texture formats (BC1-BC7), mesh export formats (FBX, OBJ), audio formats, video codecs, and shader compilation formats.

### [9.2 Xbox Platform](../subjects/09-02-Xbox-Platform.md)
Xbox-specific formats including Xbox Series X/S specific formats, Xbox One compatibility formats, texture tiling and swizzling, and XMA audio format.

### [9.3 PlayStation Platform](../subjects/09-03-PlayStation-Platform.md)
PlayStation asset guidelines including PS5 texture formats, PS4 compatibility formats, SSD optimization considerations, and Kraken compression.

### [9.4 Nintendo Switch Platform](../subjects/09-04-Nintendo-Switch-Platform.md)
Switch format recommendations including texture format recommendations, handheld mode considerations, storage format optimization, and cartridge vs download optimization.
