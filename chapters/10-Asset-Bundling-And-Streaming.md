# Chapter 10: Asset Bundling and Streaming

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Efficient asset bundling and streaming are essential for managing game content. This chapter covers strategies for organizing and delivering assets.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [10.1 AssetBundle Fundamentals](../subjects/10-01-AssetBundle-Fundamentals.md)
Core concepts of AssetBundles including bundle organization strategies, bundle dependencies, bundle compression options, Addressables system, and content catalogs.

### [10.2 Loading Strategies](../subjects/10-02-Loading-Strategies.md)
Asset loading approaches including synchronous vs asynchronous loading, scene additivity, resource unloading, memory management during loading, preloading strategies, and background loading.

### [10.3 Content Delivery](../subjects/10-03-Content-Delivery.md)
Content distribution methods including remote asset delivery, patch systems, DLC management, hot reloading, CDN integration, and version control.

### [10.4 Platform-Specific Considerations](../subjects/10-04-Platform-Specific-Considerations.md)
Platform requirements including package size limits, install vs streaming, fast storage utilization (PS5 SSD), Xbox Smart Delivery, and on-demand resources.
