# Chapter 11: Materials and Textures

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Understanding Unity's material system and texture techniques is fundamental to creating high-quality graphics.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [11.1 Material System Architecture](../subjects/11-01-Material-System-Architecture.md)
Unity's material system including material assets, material instances, material property blocks, material variants, and shared materials vs instance materials.

### [11.2 PBR Material Properties](../subjects/11-02-PBR-Material-Properties.md)
Physically-based rendering properties including albedo/base color, metallic and smoothness, normal maps, occlusion maps, emission, and height maps.

### [11.3 Texture Mapping Techniques](../subjects/11-03-Texture-Mapping-Techniques.md)
Advanced texture mapping including UV mapping, triplanar mapping, world space mapping, normal mapping, parallax mapping, detail mapping, and decals/projectors.

### [11.4 Material LOD](../subjects/11-04-Material-LOD.md)
Level of detail for materials including material quality levels, shader LOD, texture mipmap bias, and material switching strategies.

### [11.5 Advanced Texture Techniques](../subjects/11-05-Advanced-Texture-Techniques.md)
Advanced texture features including texture arrays, Texture2DArray, cubemaps and reflection, 3D textures, and texture atlasing.
