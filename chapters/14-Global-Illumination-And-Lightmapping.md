# Chapter 14: Global Illumination and Lightmapping

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Global illumination creates realistic lighting by simulating light bounces. This chapter covers baking workflows and real-time GI techniques.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [14.1 Lightmapping Fundamentals](../subjects/14-01-Lightmapping-Fundamentals.md)
Core lightmapping concepts including lightmap UV generation, lightmap resolution, lightmap compression, and directional lightmaps.

### [14.2 Baking Systems](../subjects/14-02-Baking-Systems.md)
Unity's baking solutions including Progressive Lightmapper, GPU Lightmapper, Enlighten (legacy), and baking performance optimization.

### [14.3 Light Probes and Reflection](../subjects/14-03-Light-Probes-And-Reflection.md)
Dynamic lighting solutions including light probe groups, light probe proxy volumes, reflection probes, reflection probe blending, and custom reflection sources.

### [14.4 Real-time Global Illumination](../subjects/14-04-Real-Time-Global-Illumination.md)
Real-time GI techniques including Unity real-time GI, SSGI (Screen Space GI), DDGI (Dynamic Diffuse GI), and voxel-based GI.
