# Chapter 16: Camera Systems

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Cameras are the window into your game world. This chapter covers camera configuration, multiple camera setups, and special effects.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [16.1 Camera Fundamentals](../subjects/16-01-Camera-Fundamentals.md)
Core camera concepts including projection matrices, orthographic vs perspective, field of view, clipping planes, and aspect ratio.

### [16.2 Multiple Camera Rendering](../subjects/16-02-Multiple-Camera-Rendering.md)
Multi-camera setups including camera stacking, split-screen rendering, picture-in-picture, and minimap rendering.

### [16.3 Camera Effects](../subjects/16-03-Camera-Effects.md)
Camera configuration including camera culling masks, render layers, depth texture mode, and target display settings.
