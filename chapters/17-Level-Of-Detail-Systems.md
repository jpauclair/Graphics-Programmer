# Chapter 17: Level of Detail (LOD) Systems

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

LOD systems are critical for maintaining performance with complex scenes. This chapter covers comprehensive LOD strategies for all asset types.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [17.1 LOD Fundamentals](../subjects/17-01-LOD-Fundamentals.md)
Basic LOD concepts including LOD group configuration, LOD bias and quality settings, LOD fade modes, and cross-fade transitions.

### [17.2 LOD Generation](../subjects/17-02-LOD-Generation.md)
Creating LODs including automatic LOD generation, manual LOD creation, and LOD quality guidelines.

### [17.3 Advanced LOD Techniques](../subjects/17-03-Advanced-LOD-Techniques.md)
Advanced LOD methods including billboard rendering, impostor systems, HLOD (Hierarchical LOD), and GPU-based LOD selection.

### [17.4 LOD for Specific Assets](../subjects/17-04-LOD-For-Specific-Assets.md)
Asset-specific LOD including mesh LOD, texture LOD (mipmaps), shader LOD, material LOD, and particle LOD.
