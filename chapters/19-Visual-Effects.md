# Chapter 19: Visual Effects

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Visual effects bring games to life. This chapter covers particle systems, VFX Graph, and procedural generation techniques.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

Unity's particle systems including Shuriken particle system, Visual Effect Graph (VFX Graph), GPU vs CPU particles, particle optimization, particle culling, and particle LOD.

### [19.2 VFX Graph](../subjects/19-02-VFX-Graph.md)
Advanced VFX including VFX Graph architecture, compute-based effects, custom VFX Graph blocks, performance considerations, event system, and output contexts.

### [19.3 Procedural Effects](../subjects/19-03-Procedural-Effects.md)
Procedural generation including procedural meshes, procedural textures, noise functions, and GPU-driven procedural generation.
