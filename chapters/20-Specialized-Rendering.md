# Chapter 20: Specialized Rendering

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Different content types require specialized rendering techniques. This chapter covers terrain, vegetation, water, sky, and UI rendering.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [20.1 Terrain Rendering](../subjects/20-01-Terrain-Rendering.md)
Terrain systems including heightmap-based terrain, terrain LOD systems, terrain texture splatting, terrain holes, and terrain instancing.

### [20.2 Vegetation Rendering](../subjects/20-02-Vegetation-Rendering.md)
Vegetation techniques including SpeedTree integration, billboard trees, wind animation, grass rendering, and instanced vegetation.

### [20.3 Water and Fluid Rendering](../subjects/20-03-Water-And-Fluid-Rendering.md)
Water effects including water shaders, wave simulation, reflections and refractions, underwater rendering, caustics, and flow maps.

### [20.4 Sky and Atmospheric Rendering](../subjects/20-04-Sky-And-Atmospheric-Rendering.md)
Atmospheric effects including skybox techniques, procedural sky, atmospheric scattering, volumetric clouds, weather systems, and time of day systems.

### [20.5 UI Rendering](../subjects/20-05-UI-Rendering.md)
User interface rendering including canvas rendering modes, UI batching, UI shader considerations, text rendering (TextMeshPro), UI overdraw issues, and screen space vs world space UI.
