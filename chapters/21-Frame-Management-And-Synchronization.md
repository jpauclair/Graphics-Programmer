# Chapter 21: Frame Management and Synchronization

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

Smooth frame delivery and synchronization are critical for player experience. This chapter covers frame pacing, VSync, and GPU synchronization.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [21.1 Frame Pacing](../subjects/21-01-Frame-Pacing.md)
Frame delivery control including VSync modes, frame rate limiting, variable refresh rate (VRR), and G-Sync/FreeSync support.

### [21.2 Console Frame Pacing](../subjects/21-02-Console-Frame-Pacing.md)
Console-specific frame pacing including PlayStation frame pacing, Xbox frame pacing, Switch frame pacing, and 30 FPS vs 60 FPS vs 120 FPS targets.

### [21.3 Input Latency](../subjects/21-03-Input-Latency.md)
Reducing input lag including input lag sources, frame pipelining, low latency mode, and NVIDIA Reflex integration.

### [21.4 GPU Synchronization](../subjects/21-04-GPU-Synchronization.md)
GPU synchronization primitives including fences and barriers, resource transitions, command queue synchronization, and multi-GPU rendering.
