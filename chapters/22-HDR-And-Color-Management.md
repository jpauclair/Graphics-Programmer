# Chapter 22: HDR and Color Management

[← Back to Main Index](../README.md)

High Dynamic Range and proper color management are essential for modern graphics. This chapter covers HDR pipelines and color workflows.

---

## Subjects in This Chapter

### [22.1 HDR Rendering Pipeline](../subjects/22-01-HDR-Rendering-Pipeline.md)
HDR rendering including HDR texture formats, HDR framebuffer, HDR lighting, and exposure control.

### [22.2 HDR Display Output](../subjects/22-02-HDR-Display-Output.md)
HDR display support including HDR10, Dolby Vision, platform HDR support, and HDR calibration.

### [22.3 Color Spaces](../subjects/22-03-Color-Spaces.md)
Color space management including linear vs gamma workflow, sRGB, Rec.709, Rec.2020, and DCI-P3.

### [22.4 Color Grading Workflows](../subjects/22-04-Color-Grading-Workflows.md)
Color grading techniques including LUT-based grading, lift gamma gain, color wheels, and curves/levels.
