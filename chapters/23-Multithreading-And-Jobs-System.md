# Chapter 23: Multithreading and Jobs System

Modern CPU utilization requires multithreading. This chapter covers Unity's Jobs System, Burst compiler, and ECS/DOTS for graphics.

Unity's job system including IJob interface, IJobParallelFor, job dependencies, and job safety system.

### [23.2 Burst Compiler](../subjects/23-02-Burst-Compiler.md)
High-performance compilation including Burst compilation, SIMD optimization, Mathematics package, and Burst Inspector.

### [23.3 ECS and DOTS](../subjects/23-03-ECS-And-DOTS.md)
Data-oriented design including Entity Component System, DOTS rendering, Hybrid Renderer, and Entity Graphics.

### [23.4 Thread-Safe Graphics Operations](../subjects/23-04-Thread-Safe-Graphics-Operations.md)
Multithreaded graphics including Graphics.ExecuteCommandBuffer, command buffer threading, and async GPU readback.
